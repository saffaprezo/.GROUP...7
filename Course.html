UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAM OVERVIEW

A Bachelor of Science degree in agricultural economics and agriculture engineering can be earned through CCMA programs offered in kambia district at 23 Massma Road, Kambia Two .
As an undergraduate student, the course of study can be tailored to the interests of the student by selecting a specialization in agricultural education or communication and leadership development .
Students that are interested in learning more about the specializations, need to set up an advising appointment, or have general questions related to our undergraduate programs should contact our academic advisor,Adams Fomba Bao ( adamsf@gmail.com) . Students interested in pursuing their Degree, Certificates,Hihg Diploma and Diploma delivered in Agronomy,Agriculture Engineering,Agriculture Extension Agriculture Research and Agriculture Economics. should contact Prof. Mohamed Sippo (Registrar) (profmohamedippo@gmail.com).




MINORS

Students interested in sharpening their skills within the Canadian College of Modern Agriculture discipline can also choose to select a minor through the department. Currently, CCMA offers five different courses. Additional details are provided by clicking on the name of the courses below.




  1. 1.Agriculture Economics
  2. 2.Agronomy
  3. 3.Agricultural Engineering
  4. 4.Agriculture Extension
  5. 5.Agriculture Research


Those seeking more information about the minors or needing help enrolling or registering for classes should contact the Canadian College of Modern Agriculture advisor for minors, Adams Fomba Bao ( adamsf@gmail.com).
