INTRODUCTION:

The Canadian College of Modern Agricultural Education and Communication is a group of faculty, staff and students committed to connecting people with agriculture through agricultural communication, education, leadership development and Extension education. Our college offers Certificates, Diploma, High Diploma and Degrees in agricultural education and communication for students, while also offering professional certificates for industry professionals that wish to enhance their skills in a particular area related to Agriculture. Our work as Agriculturist focus on Agronomy, Agriculture Engineering, Agriculture Extension, Agriculture Economics and research, and we hope that you will join us in connecting all people with agriculture and natural resources.



CCMA HISTORY:

Canadian College of Modern Agriculture are working on issues regarding the conservation and research of the environment, officially named the Canadian College of Modern Agriculture. CCMA was founded in 2018.



LOCATION OF CCMA:

Canadian College of Modern Agriculture is located at 23 Massma Road, Kambia Two in Kambia District.
